What is Dr.AI?

Dr.AI is an advanced healthcare recommendation system that utilizes cutting-edge artificial intelligence technology to provide personalized medical advice and diagnosis suggestions based on your symptoms.

By fine-tuning OpenAI GPT 3.5-turbo model on a vast dataset of medical information, Dr.AI can understand and interpret your symptoms, then recommend the most appropriate course of action, whether it's seeking a particular specialist or considering potential illnesses.
